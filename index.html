Say Goodbye to Messy Rooms

With HostelSync, schedule your hostel room cleaning anytime using just a QR code!

Problem

Hostel cleaning is usually done on weekends when residents may not be available. This leads to missed cleaning slots, inconvenience, and unhygienic conditions. Traditional schedules lack flexibility for students with different routines.

Solution

Scan the QR code placed in your hostel, choose a preferred cleaning time, and a cleaner will arrive as scheduled. HostelSync provides flexibility and convenience for hostel residents.

Benefits & Implementation

  • Personalized scheduling for each resident
  • Organized cleaner workflow
  • Reduces missed cleaning opportunities
